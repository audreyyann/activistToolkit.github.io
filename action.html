How can I improve my relationship with social media?

Short-term Solutions

1. Taking frequent breaks from social media to recharge and focus on self care

2. Picking up new hobbies to prevent dependency on social media

3. Reaching out to friends and building meaningful relationships

Long-term Solutions

1. Seeking professional help, such as therapy

2. Avoiding habitual or mindless scrolling

3. Avoiding hyperfixation on the number of likes or follows
