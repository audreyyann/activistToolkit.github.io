What are the detrimental effects of social media on teen mental health?

Social media harbors a very pretentious facade of “perfection”, which causes teens to…

1. Feel insecure about the productivity of their own lives and experience FOMO (e.g. "That girl" trend), loneliness, or jealousy

2. Experience body dysmorphia or eating disorders, exacerbated by photoshopped selfies, flattering makeup and camera angles, and filters

Consuming media typically leads to a sedentary lifestyle and addiction, which causes teens to…

1. Feel a drop in “happy” hormone levels, such as serotonin, leading to depression

2. Miss out on real life experiences with loved ones

3. Spend less time learning and improving themselves

Exposure to cyber bullying

And this is only scratching the surface of the link between social media and mental health. Do your own research as well!
